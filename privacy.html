Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

At Hellas Aalborg, we respect your privacy. This page explains how we handle information on our virtual showroom.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal identification information (such as names or emails) directly through this 3D experience. However, we use Google Analytics to track general usage statistics (e.g., number of visitors, time spent on site) to improve your experience.

2. Third-Party Services

When you click on buttons to order via Wolt or our web app, you will be redirected to their respective platforms. These third parties have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

3. Cookies

Our analytics service may use cookies to provide a better understanding of site traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at Ved Stranden 21, 9000 Aalborg or call us at +45 42 17 77 54.
